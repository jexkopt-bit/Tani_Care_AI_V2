## Team Name: Jexko-1Bit
**Project Title:** TaniCare AI – Kawan Petani, Penjaga Tanaman
**Hackathon:** MyAI Future Hackathon 2026 – Track 1: Padi & Plates (Agrotech & Food Security)
Problem
Malaysia’s rice self-sufficiency ratio is only ~52% in 2026, far below the NAP 2.0 target of 80%. Smallholder farmers (padi, sawit, sayur, buah) lose 30–70% of yield every season due to late detection of crop diseases. Limited access to agronomists and lack of real-time tools worsen food import dependency and threaten farmer livelihoods.
Solution
TaniCare AI is a smart, farmer-friendly mobile app that enables instant crop disease detection and advanced monitoring.
Key Features

One-tap photo diagnosis using Google Gemini 1.5 Pro (accurate results in Bahasa Melayu)
Advanced satellite analysis: NDVI + EVI + anomaly detection via Google Earth Engine
Real-time weather integration (temperature, humidity, rain) for intelligent risk alerts
Professional Analytics Dashboard with interactive NDVI trend charts and NDVI vs EVI bar comparison
Dynamic support for all Malaysian states
Scan history automatically saved to Google Cloud Firestore

## Technology Stack

**Frontend:** Flutter + Riverpod 2.0 (advanced state management)
**AI:** Google Gemini 1.5 Pro
**Satellite:** Google Earth Engine (Sentinel-2)
**Weather:** Open-Meteo real-time API
**Database:** Google Cloud Firestore (GCP)
**Charts:** fl_chart
**Backend:** Google Cloud Functions (Python)

## Impact

Expected to reduce crop yield losses by 30–50%s
Directly supports DKMN 2030 and NAP 2.0 (smart agriculture & farmer welfare)
Lowers national food import bill
Scalable, offline-capable, and designed for real smallholder farmers
